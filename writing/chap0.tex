\title{XAI Machine Learning-Based Fault Classification of Power Transformers Using Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA)}

%\TitleMalay{KLASIFIKASI KEROSAKAN TRANSFORMER KUASA MENGGUNAKAN PEMBELAJARAN MESIN BERASASKAN DGA}

\abstract{Power transformers are critical components in electrical power systems, and their failure can lead to significant operational disruptions and economic losses. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) is a widely used technique for detecting incipient faults by analyzing gas concentrations such as hydrogen (H$_2$), methane (CH$_4$), and acetylene (C$_2$H$_2$). However, traditional diagnostic methods are rule-based and often limited in handling complex fault patterns and overlapping conditions.
This study proposes an Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) machine learning framework for transformer fault classification using DGA data. The proposed approach focuses on three key aspects: developing a high-accuracy classification model, addressing data-related challenges such as class imbalance and limited labeled data, and ensuring interpretability through explainability techniques. Advanced machine learning algorithms are employed for classification, while data preprocessing and feature engineering techniques are used to enhance model robustness.
Furthermore, SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP) is integrated to provide feature-level explanations, ensuring that model predictions are aligned with physical fault mechanisms. The proposed framework aims to achieve a balance between accuracy, robustness, and interpretability, making it suitable for real-world engineering applications in power system diagnostics.}

\abstractMalay{Transformer kuasa merupakan komponen kritikal dalam sistem tenaga elektrik, dan kegagalannya boleh menyebabkan gangguan operasi serta kerugian ekonomi yang besar. Analisis Gas Terlarut (DGA) merupakan kaedah yang digunakan secara meluas untuk mengesan kerosakan awal dengan menganalisis kepekatan gas seperti hidrogen (H$_2$), metana (CH$_4$), dan asetilena (C$_2$H$_2$). Walau bagaimanapun, kaedah tradisional adalah berasaskan peraturan dan mempunyai keterbatasan dalam menangani corak kerosakan yang kompleks.
Kajian ini mencadangkan satu rangka kerja pembelajaran mesin berasaskan Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) untuk klasifikasi kerosakan transformer menggunakan data DGA. Pendekatan ini memberi tumpuan kepada tiga aspek utama iaitu pembangunan model berketepatan tinggi, pengendalian cabaran data seperti ketidakseimbangan kelas dan data terhad, serta memastikan kebolehjelasan melalui teknik explainability.
Tambahan pula, kaedah SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP) digunakan untuk memberikan penjelasan pada tahap ciri, sekaligus memastikan keputusan model selaras dengan mekanisme fizikal kerosakan. Rangka kerja ini bertujuan mencapai keseimbangan antara ketepatan, kebolehpercayaan, dan kebolehjelasan dalam aplikasi sebenar sistem tenaga.}
